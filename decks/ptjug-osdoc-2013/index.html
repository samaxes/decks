JUG Benefits

Benefits for individual members

  • Get the latest information on Java technology and how to implement it
  • Have access to experts in their field. If they are experts, they have access to an audience to share their skills
  • Learn how to publicly speak and communicate ideas

Benefits for companies

  • Sponsoring a JUG offers opportunities for free advertising
  • When looking to hire new people, companies who have a JUG presence get top picks
